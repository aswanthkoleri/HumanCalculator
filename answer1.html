Answer Is 96
12 months in a year, 52 weeks in a year, and 365 days in a year Let m be my age in years. If s is my son's age in years, then my son is 52s weeks old. If g is my grandson's age in years, then my grandson is 365g days old. Thus, 365g = 52s. Since my grandson is 12g months old, 12g = m. Since my grandson, my son and I together are 160 years, g + s + m = 160. The above system of 3 equations in 3 unknowns (g, s and m) can be solved as follows. m / 12 + 365 m / (52 × 12) + m = 160 or 52 m + 365 m + 624 m = 624 × 160 or m = 624 × 160 / 1041 = 96. So, I am 96 years old."
